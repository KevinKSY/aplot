|uint32_in| **Color**
    Text color (PlotColor or LabView color).
    
|uint32_in| **Background Color**
    Color of text background (box directly behind the text.)  PlotColor or
    LabView color.
    
|int32_in| **Font Size**
    Size of text.  Choosing a relative size (Small, Medium, Large, etc.) from
    the drop-down is recommended.  You can also choose "Other" and specify
    an absolute font size (in points) manually.
    
|enum_in| **Font Style**
    Style of font (normal, italic, bold).
    
|double_in| **Rotation angle**
    Angle of rotation (about left bottom corner).
